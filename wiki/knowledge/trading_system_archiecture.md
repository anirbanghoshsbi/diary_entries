# Multi-Strategy Trading System

## Core Idea
Edge = small advantage × consistency × scale

## Strategies
1. Trend (Elder Impulse) — core engine
2. Mean Reversion — options-based
3. Volatility Expansion — breakout system
4. Carry — options spreads

## Rules
- No cross-interference
- No discretionary overrides
- Predefined playbooks only

## Allocation
- Trend: 40%
- Mean Reversion: 15%
- Vol Expansion: 15%
- Carry: 20%
- Cash: 10%

## Key Constraint
Uncorrelated strategies via payoff, not assets
