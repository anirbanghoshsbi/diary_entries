# Trading Rules

## Elder Impulse System
- Green: EMA rising + MACD rising
- Red: EMA falling + MACD falling

## Core Rules
- Never short on green bar
- Add on red bars
- Stop loss: close above 9 EMA

## Moving Average Reality
- MAs work due to institutional behavior
- Support = collective action
- Role reversal is critical concept

## Linda Raschke Principles
- Trend continuation > reversal
- Momentum precedes price
- Trends end in climax
- Expansion ↔ contraction cycles
