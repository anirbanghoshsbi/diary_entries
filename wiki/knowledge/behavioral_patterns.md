# Behavioral Patterns (Critical)

## Trading Self-Sabotage
- Breaks rules after entering trades
- Fiddles with positions out of boredom
- Switches to options after profitable phase
- Attempts to “protect gains” → loses edge

## Core Problem
- Discomfort with sustained success
- Preference for recovery over stability

## Required Rules
- No in-trade modification
- No switching instruments mid-strategy
- System integrity > tactical cleverness

## Key Reminder
If you override the system → you destroy the edge
