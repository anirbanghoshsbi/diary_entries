# 🦵 Leg Day Protocol

## Structure
- Warm-up
- Strength
- Stability
- Power

## Exercises
- Goblet squat
- RDL
- Step-ups
- Bulgarian split squat
- Glute bridge
- Calf raise
- Sit-to-stand

## Rules
- Start with right side
- Add +2 reps to weaker side
- RPE 6–7
- Stop on pain

## Progression
- +2.5 kg OR reps
