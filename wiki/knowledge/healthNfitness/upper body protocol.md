# 🏋️ Upper Body Protocol

## Constraints
- Neutral grip
- No overhead work
- Pain-free ROM

## Exercises
- Single-arm floor press
- Single-arm row
- Chest-supported row
- Face pull
- Farmer carry
- Dead bug

## Avoid
- Overhead press
- Heavy bench
- Wide push-ups

## Principle
Joint safety > load
