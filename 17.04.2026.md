- 09:00 # 🦵 Leg Day Routine (60 Minutes) – Customized

## 🎯 Objective
- Build strength + symmetry
- Correct right-side weakness (hemiparesis)
- Protect joints (shoulder + spine)
- Improve balance and control

---

## ⏱ Total Time: 60 min
- Warm-up: 10 min
- Strength: 25 min
- Stability: 15 min
- Power: 5–7 min

---

## 🔥 1. Neuromuscular Warm-up (10 min)

- Single-leg balance (Right focus) → 3 × 20 sec
- Clamshells → 15 reps each side
- Hip hinge drill → 15 reps
- Slow bodyweight squats → 10 reps (5 sec down)

---

## 🏋️ 2. Strength Block (25 min)

### Goblet Squat
- Weight: 16–20 kg
- Sets: 3
- Reps: 10
- Tempo: 3 sec down

### Romanian Deadlift (RDL)
- Weight: 30–40 kg
- Sets: 3
- Reps: 10

### Step-Ups
- Weight: Bodyweight or 5–8 kg dumbbells
- Sets: 2
- Reps: 10 each leg

---

## 🧱 3. Stability + Correction (15 min)

### Bulgarian Split Squat
- Weight: Bodyweight or 5–6 kg
- Sets: 2
- Reps: 8 each leg

### Glute Bridge
- Weight: Bodyweight or 10–20 kg
- Sets: 2
- Reps: 15
- Hold: 2 sec at top

### Standing Calf Raise
- Weight: Bodyweight + 10 kg
- Sets: 3
- Reps: 15–20

---

## ⚡ 4. Controlled Power (5–7 min)

### Sit-to-Stand (Chair Squat)
- Sets: 3
- Reps: 8
- Tempo: Explosive up, slow down

---

## ⚠️ Key Rules

### Right-Side Bias
- Start all unilateral exercises with right leg
- Add +2 reps on right side if imbalance exists

### Pain Rule
- Stop if sharp pain
- Reduce load if shoulder discomfort appears

### Fatigue Rule
- Train at RPE 6–7 (not failure)

---

## 📈 Progression

- Increase weight only if:
  - Form is stable
  - Movement is symmetrical

- Progression method:
  - +2.5 kg OR
  - +1–2 reps per week

---

## 🧠 Focus Cues

- Slow, controlled movement
- Equal weight distribution
- Stability > intensity
- Consistency > exhaustion

- Weight: 6–10 kg
- Sets: 3
- Reps: 8–10 each side

### Single-Arm Dumbbell Row (Supported)

- Sets: 3
- Reps: 10 each side

- Sets: 2
- Reps: 12

- Time: 30 sec each side × 3

---

### Face Pull (Band/Cable)
- Weight: Light
- Sets: 2
- Reps: 15

---

### Hammer Curl (Neutral Grip)
- Weight: 4–6 kg
- Sets: 2
- Reps: 10

---

## ⚡ 4. Core (5–8 min)

### Dead Bug
- Sets: 3
- Reps: 10 slow reps

---

## ❌ Avoid

- Overhead press
- Heavy barbell bench press
- Wide grip push-ups
- Straight bar curls
- Any painful movement

---

  - No pain
  - Good control
  - Symmetry maintained

  - +1–2 kg OR

### Right-Side Priority
- Start all unilateral exercises with right side
- Add +2 reps if right side is weaker

---

### Pain Rule
- Stop immediately if sharp pain
- Modify or reduce load if discomfort appears

---

### Fatigue Rule
- Train at RPE 6–7
- Avoid failure

---

## 🧠 Focus

- Control > weight
- Stability > intensity
- Consistency > exhaustion
